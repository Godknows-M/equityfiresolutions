Our Services

A comprehensive suite of fire protection and related commercial services, providing a depth and breadth of technical expertise that few companies in the industry can match.

Equity Fire Solutions specialises in the sales, supply, design, installation, commissioning, servicing and maintenance of fire detection and alarm systems, fire suppression systems, security and access control systems, industrial and commercial plumbing and air conditioning systems. Our integrated capability allows us to manage the complete system lifecycle from concept and design through to installation, commissioning and long term maintenance.

Design & Engineering

Fire Systems Design and Engineering

Our design services deliver accurate, compliant and construction ready fire protection drawings that meet the requirements of reviewers, authorities, stocklisters and installers.

Designs are fully dimensioned and include main and branch line identification, precise cut lengths on grooved mains, hanger locations and coordination details. This ensures efficient installation, reduced rework and full regulatory approval.

  • Accurate, Compliant Drawings
  • Fully Dimensioned Specifications
  • Main & Branch Line Identification
  • Precise Cut Lengths & Hanger Locations
  • Full Regulatory Approval
