Who We Are

Customer Centric Fire & Life Safety

Equity Fire Solutions is a customer centric, technology driven fire and life safety company delivering intelligent, compliant and integrated protection systems. We combine professional expertise, smart technology and people first safety to protect life, property and business continuity.

Established in 2017, Equity Fire Solutions Private Limited specialises in the sales, supply, design, installation, commissioning, servicing and maintenance of fire detection and alarm systems, fire suppression systems, security and access control systems, industrial and commercial plumbing and air conditioning systems.

All works are executed by industry qualified trades and technical personnel supported by approved specialist subcontractors, ensuring precision, accountability and compliance at every stage.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Aim

Our guiding principles that drive every decision and every project we undertake.

Our Mission

To design, deliver and maintain high quality, intelligent fire and safety solutions through professional excellence, advanced technology and uncompromising standards, creating long term value and trust for every client.

Our Vision

To lead the future of fire protection, where intelligent systems detect faster, respond smarter and suppress fire before destruction occurs. A future where fire is controlled, lives are protected and safety is engineered by design.

Our Aim

To eliminate fire risk through precision engineering, proactive maintenance and data driven safety systems, while placing people, reliability and quality at the center of every solution.
